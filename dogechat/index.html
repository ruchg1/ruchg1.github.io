Dogechat User Guide

What is DogeChat?

DogeChat is an online chatroom that rewards constructive conversation with Dogecoins. Hint: The mods don't like referral links or copypasta.

You'll meet some interesting people while Dogechatting.

Popular Channels

  • #main, for logical conversation.
  • #coding, for those who code.
  • #radiofornax, a bot-based internet radio station.
  • #mods, if you need to talk to the mods.
  • #offtopic, for random conversation.

Basic Rules

  • No trolling or reflinks.
  • No NSFW unless the room you're posting in allows it.
  • The mods are always right.
  • No alt accounts.
  • Last, but not least, no spamming.

Bots

You can run a bot at DogeChat if you follow some basic rules:

  • You must register the bot with chi.
  • The bot can only be in YOUR room.
  • The bot can only post two unprovoked messages.

Violation of these rules can lead to your bot (and possibly you) being banned.

Commands

  • /me <message>: This command allows you to say you are doing something. e.g. RichG is petting a cat
  • /tip <user> <amount> <optional message>: Tip doges to others with this command.
  • /kick <user>: Kick users from a channel (only works if you are an op in that channel)
  • /op <optional user>: List ops in a channel. If you are an op in a channel, you can add ops to that channel with this command.
  • /deop <user>: Remove a user's op status in your channel (only works if you're opped in that channel).

Snapping

Dogechat has a way for you to put your face into the chat (literally). All you need to do is follow these directions:

  • If your browser gives you a warning, click "Allow".
  • Press the "Snap" button.
  • Type what you want in the chat box (keeping the Snap window open), and press enter.

*Now your face is literally in the chat!

*Please do not snap custom images.
